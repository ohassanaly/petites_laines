Hi, I'm the maker behind Cozy Stitches

I knit and sew small-batch accessories with a focus on warmth, comfort, and simple, timeless style.

My story

I started knitting as a hobby and fell in love with the craft. Over the years, I refined my techniques and began creating pieces for friends and family. Now I offer a rotating selection of handmade items ready to ship.

Each piece is made by hand using carefully chosen yarns and fabrics. I love experimenting with textures and patterns that are cozy to wear and easy to care for.
